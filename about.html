I'm Alyssa
Junior User Interface Engineer
I'm a 22-year-old UI/UX geek living in NYC. I currently work for a small audio-visual company, and in my spare time I'm a freelance developer.

In addition to UI/UX, I have a pretty solid background in programming. I started to learn HTML when I was in middle school by reverse engineering MySpace layouts. From there, I dove into JavaScript and that's when I really began making my own websites, small apps, etc.

However, user interface design is by far my favorite thing about tech today. I totally geek out over clean, modern interfaces. Likewise, I cringe when I see outdated, archiac systems that make no sense (cough, cough MTA). I'm always carrying around a small notebook in my bag where I jot down problems I see and possible solutions for them. After my work day, I can be found re-designing/re-developing those issues into easier, more intuitive renditions. Then, I put them here, on my portfolio!
